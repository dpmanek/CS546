The Tale of Naruto Uzumaki

Once there lived a boy named Naruto. He was an Orphan. He was raised by the hokage of Leaf Village. People in his village resented him because spirit of nine tailed fox was sealed inside of him. He was a child to understand why people treated him this way, as he knew nothing about how Kurama the nine tailed fox had reaked havoc upon this village few years ago. This silly boy needed nothing but respect and attention, so he decided to be the greatest and best amongst all. He wanted to become the greatest ninja of all time.

His journey is full of amazing experiences. Along these experiences he gained many friends namely

1 Sasuke Uchiha
2 Sakura Haruno
3 Jiraiya the Gallant

Eventually with them he embarked on amazing life turning journey and one day he is successful in overcoming harships and many villains by fighting an insane and epic battles.

Doing so he ends up being a hokage (Leader of his village). Loved by everybody
